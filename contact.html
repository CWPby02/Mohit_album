FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery is 3–5 business days after design approval. We also offer express 24-hour delivery for select album types.

Do you deliver outside my city?

Yes! We deliver pan-India. All albums are carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery.

Can I see a design preview before printing?

Absolutely. We send a complete digital preview via WhatsApp for your approval before we print. Unlimited revisions until you're happy.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, Bank Transfer, PhonePe, Paytm, and cash on delivery (for local orders). We take 50% advance and 50% on delivery.

Do you offer bulk order discounts?

Yes! For orders of 5 or more albums, we offer special pricing. WhatsApp us for a custom bulk quote.
